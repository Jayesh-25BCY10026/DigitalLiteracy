Do’s:
Think before posting, make sure it’s appropriate and not offensive
Try to protect your privacy by using strong passwords and settings
Always verify information before sharing it further
Be respectful while interacting with others online
Use social media for learning, networking and positive things 


Don’ts:
Don’t share personal details like phone number, address or passwords
Don’t post anything harmful, offensive or inappropriate
Don’t blindly trust or forward unverified news
Don’t get involved in cyberbullying or unnecessary arguments
Don’t overshare things that might affect your image later
