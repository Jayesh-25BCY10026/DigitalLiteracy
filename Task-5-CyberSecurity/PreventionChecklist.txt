PREVENTION CHECKLIST:

    1.> Use strong and different passwords for each account (don’t keep same everywhere)
    2.> Turn on 2-factor authentication (2FA) on apps like Gmail, Insta, banking etc
    3.> Avoid clicking on unknown links in emails, WhatsApp or SMS, even if it looks urgent
    4.> Always check the website URL properly before entering your details
    5.> Check QR codes before scanning, some of them can be fake
    6.> Use only trusted payment apps, don’t install random apps from unknown sources
    7.> Never share your UPI PIN, OTP or bank details with anyone (banks usually don’t ask this)
    If Something Goes Wrong;
        8.> Report fraud on National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
        9.> Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 as soon as possible
